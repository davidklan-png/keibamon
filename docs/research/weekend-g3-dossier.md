# Race dossier — Jun 28 G3s (as of Thu early-bird odds)

Awareness material, not a tip. None of this signals a winning bet — it surfaces
*patterns to be aware of* as you decide where to put your money. Odds below are
Thursday-night early-bird (early pool = noisy; the real money forms Sunday). The
live plunge/cross-pool scan runs Sunday as the pool fills — this is the baseline
to watch it against.

> **What "unusual" looks like** (from the year-long drift study): a **chalk
> horse (<6.0)** moving more than ~25% by post is notable; **mid-price (6–12)**
> routinely moves ±20–35%; **longshots (30+)** swing ±85% as noise — a longshot
> drifting is meaningless, a *favorite* collapsing or ballooning is the signal.
