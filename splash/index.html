Keibamon 競馬モン

A local-first data & machine-learning lab for Japanese horse racing (JRA) — built to turn open racing data into honest, testable insight. The point isn't a tip sheet. It's a yardstick rigorous enough to tell a real signal from a flattering coincidence — and the willingness to publish what it says.

PROGRAMME COMPLETE · 6 NULLS · MARKET EFFICIENT

// The platform

DATA LAKE
30 yrs

Medallion lake

JRA-VAN official records normalized bronze→silver→gold: ~462k race entries, plus a full year of intraday odds curves (83M time-series rows), training times (12.5M), mining, going & weather.

INTEGRITY
PIT

The evaluation spine

Point-in-time correct (a decision at time t sees only what was knowable at t), leakage-guarded, walk-forward, settled at official payouts, judged against a calibrated market baseline. The yardstick everything is measured against.

LIVE
race-day

Capture & dashboard

Official realtime odds captured race-day, residual-drift & going context surfaced to a phone dashboard. Informational only — never a bet recommender.

// How it runs — the architecture

TOPOLOGY
one host

Local-first, single machine

Consolidating onto one stationary workstation: ingestion, the medallion lake, modeling, race-day capture and the edge push all run on the same local machine — no cloud database of record, no cross-machine handoff. The git repo and the local lake are the only sources of truth.

DATA FLOW
source → spine → phone

Lake to edge

Race data lands as immutable bronze, normalizes through silver→gold→marts in DuckDB, and is judged by the evaluation spine. Only curated, derived results push to a throwaway Cloudflare D1 + Worker that a phone reads at the track — if the edge goes stale, the lake is still the truth.

MIGRATION
in progress

Retiring the licensed feed

The original two-machine design — a Windows JV-Link host feeding the analytics machine over an airgap — is collapsing to a single, scrape-sourced host. The licensed historical pull is frozen as an immutable cross-check oracle; the new feed must reconcile against it at zero mismatch before the old host is switched off.

Six signals tested, six honest nulls. Each was held to the same bar — beat the de-vigged market, out of sample, net of takeout, with the confidence interval clearing zero. The capstone is the cleanest: a full multivariate model that genuinely predicts in isolation (it learns real handicapping signal), yet contributes exactly zero once the market price is in the blend — the market had already priced every scrap. The JRA pari-mutuel prices public information efficiently, across static factors, intraday dynamics, and cross-pool projection. These negative results are the most valuable thing here: knowing the market is efficient beats paying to find out. The public-information edge-hunt is closed.

// What the data did reveal

STRUCTURE

Henery γ ≈ 0.856

Favorites are less dominant in 2nd/3rd than naive Harville pricing assumes — reproduced walk-forward, matching the racing-statistics literature (Henery 1981 / Stern 1994).

STRUCTURE

Favorite-longshot bias compounds

In exotics, popular combinations are overbet and thin longshot combos underbet — boxing favorites systematically overpays. A "bet-smarter" nuance, not an edge.

WHY IT'S TRUSTWORTHY

Market is calibrated — even the tail

The de-vigged win market is near-perfectly calibrated (no exploitable favorite-longshot mispricing). That sound baseline is precisely why the nulls hold up.
