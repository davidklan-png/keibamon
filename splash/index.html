Keibamon 競馬モン

Your companion for Japanese horse racing (JRA) — weekend graded-stakes research that refreshes through post time, horse-and-jockey form at a glance, and honest ticket structuring you can copy straight onto the JRA card. Built on a research spine rigorous enough to prove the market is efficient — which is exactly why it will never sell you a winning system.

// What it does

WEEKEND ROUNDUP

Graded-stakes research, live

Every G1–G3 on the card, with race shape, contenders, pace, gate & going — and it refreshes itself every few minutes through post time, with an "as of" stamp so you always know how current the odds are.

FORM & YOUR READ

Know the runners

Tap any horse for career, recent finishes with surface & distance, going splits and running style — then mark your own read (anchor, like, price, avoid) and watch it against how the market moves.

HONEST TICKETS

Structure, then transcribe

Turn your reads into box / wheel / formation tickets across 馬連・ワイド・3連複・馬単・3連単, with a mark-card fill guide you copy straight onto the JRA card. The math is honest — WIDE pays on multiple lines, and we count every one.

GLOSSARY

The card, decoded

A bilingual EN / 日本語 reference for the terms, bet types and track language — so the betting card stops being a wall of kanji and starts making sense.

// Why you can trust it

Six signals tested, six honest nulls. Before any of this was a companion, it was a research lab. Every promising edge — data-mining, going aptitude, training times, the odds curve, cross-pool exotics, even a full multivariate model — was held to one bar: beat the de-vigged market, out of sample, net of takeout. All six failed it. The JRA pari-mutuel prices public information efficiently. We publish that finding plainly, because a companion that knows it can't sell you an edge is one you can actually trust.

INTEGRITY
PIT

Point-in-time honest

A decision at time t sees only what was knowable at t — leakage-guarded, walk-forward, settled at official payouts, judged against a calibrated market baseline. The same rigor underwrites the live numbers.

NEVER A TIP SHEET
your call

No picks, no rankings

Keibamon surfaces data and structures your read. It never names a winner, ranks runners, or claims a profit. The decision is always yours — and the takeout is always shown.

CALIBRATED
even the tail

The market is the yardstick

The de-vigged win market is near-perfectly calibrated, with no exploitable favorite-longshot mispricing. That sound baseline is precisely why the honest numbers in the app hold up.

// How it runs

LOCAL-FIRST

Lake to edge

A medallion data lake (bronze→silver→gold) of JRA records and intraday odds curves lives on one machine and is the source of truth. Only curated, derived results push to a Cloudflare edge — if the edge goes stale, the lake is still right.

SELF-REFRESHING

The Roundup publishes itself

On race day a scheduled job rebuilds the weekend report from the live capture every few minutes — no human in the loop. A staleness guard freezes it honestly if the upstream feed ever stalls, rather than faking freshness.

OPEN

Built in public

122 commits in the first 17 days, the architecture decisions written down as ADRs, and the negative results published alongside the wins. The code and the reasoning are both in the open.
