// The app · アプリでできること

WEEKEND ROUNDUP

Graded-stakes research, live

Every G1–G3 on the card — race shape, contenders, pace, gate & going — and it refreshes itself every few minutes through post time, with an "as of" stamp so you always know how current the odds are.

RUNNERS & YOUR READ

Know the horses

Tap any horse for career form, recent finishes with surface & distance, and running style — then mark your own read (anchor, like, price, avoid) and watch it against how the market moves.

HONEST TICKETS

Structure, then transcribe

Turn your reads into box / wheel / formation tickets across 馬連・ワイド・3連複・馬単・3連単, drawn like real track tickets. A mark-card fill guide copies straight onto the JRA card, and the cost & fair-payout math is honest — WIDE pays on multiple lines, and we count every one.

FRIENDS & SHARINGNEW

Share the race day

Add friends by handle or invite link, share a ticket with everyone or just a few friends, and comment on each other's tickets. When a friend's ticket wins, it surfaces on its own with a congratulations, and a bell keeps track of what's new.

Nothing is visible to friends until you share it. · シェアするまで友だちには見えません。
GLOSSARY

The card, decoded

A bilingual EN / 日本語 reference for the terms, bet types and track language — so the betting card stops being a wall of kanji and starts making sense.

YOUR TICKETS

Saved, settled, tracked

Your tickets live in one place — saved ahead of the race, edited or deleted until the off, then settled at the official payout with your wins tracked across the weekend.

// The technology · なぜ信頼できるか

The companion is built on a research lab that held itself to one bar — beat the market on races it had never seen. That rigor is why the live numbers hold up, and it's also why you'll never see a tip in this app.

THE DATA SPINE

One lake, curated to the edge

A medallion data lake (bronze→silver→gold) of JRA records and intraday odds lives on one machine and is the source of truth; only curated results push to the Cloudflare edge. Official JV-Link data feeds it, a backup capture guards it, and if the edge ever goes stale, a staleness guard freezes the numbers honestly instead of faking fresh.

POINT-IN-TIME HONESTY
PIT

What you knew, when you knew it

A decision at time t sees only what was knowable at t — leakage-guarded, walk-forward, and settled at the official payout. The same rule underwrites the live numbers you see on race day.

WHY THERE ARE NO TIPS
6 / 6

The market is efficient

Before any of this was a companion, it was a research lab. Six promising edges were each held to one bar — beat the market's fair odds on races it had never seen, after the track's takeout. All six failed it. The JRA pari-mutuel prices public information efficiently, so this app surfaces data and structures your read. It never names a winner. Never a tip sheet.
